Welcome to the new site. I rebuilt it from the ground up with [Astro](https://astro.build),
keeping the neumorphic look I'd been sketching and finally giving myself somewhere to write.

## Why Astro

I wanted something that ships as static HTML by default, stays fast, and gets
out of the way when I just want to publish a page. Astro's content collections
make a blog feel like editing files — which is exactly what I wanted.

## What's next

A few things I want to write about:

- Building products end to end at **IFF Productions**
- Notes from photography and music — the non-code disciplines
- The occasional deep-dive on something I had to figure out the hard way

More soon.
